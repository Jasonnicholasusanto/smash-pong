Welcome to Smash Pong!

Smash Pong! A remake of the original Pong video game but with a twist!


Game Options

  • 1 v 1: Player versus Player

  • 1 v PC: Player versus Computer

  • PC v PC: Computer versus Computer

Player Controls

  • Red paddle movements: "w" to move up and "s" to move down.

  • Red paddle smash: "t" to smash the ball.

  • Blue paddle movements: "ArrowUp" to move up and "ArrowDown" to move down.

  • Blue paddle smash: "Enter" to smash the ball.

Objective & Rules

  • Score the ball on your opponent's side to win

  • When the ball is heading towards your bat, smash it to return the ball at a higher speed to your opponent's side

  • A duel is played at a best-of-five format and a maximum round of 5 games.

  • Have fun!
